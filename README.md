📊 **AtliQ Hardware – Sales & Finance Analysis (Excel)**

📌 **Project Overview**

This project analyzes AtliQ Hardware's global sales and financial performance using Microsoft Excel.
The objective is to uncover business insights across markets, customers, products, and financial metrics to support data-driven decision making.

The analysis includes Net Sales, Gross Margin, Product Performance, Market Performance, and Customer Sales Trends across multiple fiscal years.

The dashboard and reports help stakeholders identify high-growth markets, top performing products, and revenue drivers.

🎯 **Business Objectives**

The project answers the following business questions:

How has sales performance evolved over time?

Which markets and countries drive the highest revenue?

Which products contribute most to growth?

How has profitability changed across years?

Which customers generate the highest revenue?

📌 **Key Business Insights**

India is the largest revenue contributor, accounting for over $161M in sales.

Rapid sales expansion between 2020 and 2021 (3× growth) indicates aggressive market expansion.

High-growth products drove revenue acceleration, particularly in computing and peripherals categories.

Despite revenue growth, gross margin percentage slightly declined, indicating increased costs or pricing strategies.

🚀 **Project Outcome**

This analysis provides a clear view of revenue drivers, product performance, and market opportunities that can help the company:

Optimize product strategy

Improve pricing and profitability

Identify high-growth markets

Focus on top revenue generating customers
